research_direction: 心脏疾病与营养补充  
other_info: The study aims to evaluate the long-term effects of statins in high-risk cardiovascular patients. It is a randomised, placebo-controlled trial comparing 80 mg daily atorvastatin versus placebo for 2 years. Exclusion criteria include asthma patients and those with uncontrolled diabetes or kidney disease (Stage I-III). The primary outcome measures include major adverse cardiovascular events (MACE), defined as non-fatal myocardial infarction, non-fatal stroke, fatal myocardial infarction, or fatal stroke. Secondary outcomes include cardiovascular death, non-cardiovascular death, and all-cause mortality.

research_direction: Efficacy of Folic Acid Therapy in Primary Prevention of Stroke Among Adults With Hypertension in China  
other_info: This study aims to evaluate the effectiveness of folic acid therapy in the primary prevention of stroke among adults with hypertension in China. The research will be conducted as a randomised, double-blind, placebo-controlled trial involving 1000 participants. Inclusion criteria include adults aged 30-60 years with a diagnosis of hypertension and no history of stroke orTIA (within the past one year). Exclusion criteria include individuals with contraindications to folic acid therapy, such as pregnancy, liver disease, or a history of allergic reactions. The primary outcome measure is the reduction in stroke incidence after 12 months of follow-up. Secondary outcomes include changes in risk factors for hypertension and cognitive function assessments.
