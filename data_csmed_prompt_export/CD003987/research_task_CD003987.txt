research_direction: Systematic Review on Contraceptive Drug Effects  
other_info: The review found no substantial difference in weight change between different combination contraceptives and identified that discontinuation due to weight change did not differ between groups. The evidence was insufficient to determine a causal association but suggested no large effect. This systematic review includes studies comparing the combined contraceptive ring, NuvaRing, with an oral contraceptive containing 30 g ethinyl estradiol and 3 mg drospirenone for their effects on cycle control and bodyweight.  

Effects on cycle control and bodyweight of the combined contraceptive ring, NuvaRing, versus an oral contraceptive containing 30 g ethinyl estradiol and 3 mg drospirenone
