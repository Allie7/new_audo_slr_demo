research_direction: Use of tocolytic agents, reverse breech extraction, and forceps/vacuum in caesarean section  
other_info: 研究将纳入两年内进行的非随机对照试验，比较使用任意活检药物或选择性活检药物与不使用药物或安慰剂；反车头提取与头推法在obstructed labour中的对比；使用力断器或真空辅助分娩时与不使用工具的对比。研究对象为接受剖腹产的妇女及胎儿，评估指标包括婴儿出生创伤、血流情况、Apgar评分等。纳入标准为两年内进行的非随机对照试验，比较药物使用与否和辅助方法效果；排除标准为quasi-randomised trials（非随机试验）。结果 prestated by review authors only. Search methods used in previous version of the review included searching the Cochrane Central Register of Controlled Trials (Cochrane Library 2006, Issue 1) and PubMed (1966 to January 2006) using keywords such as cesarean section; tocolysis; betamimetics; glyceryl tri‐nitrate; calcium channel blockers; birth injury; and birth trauma.
