research_direction: Research comparing rHuEPO with other interventions in predialysis patients with renal anaemia, including those who have not yet commenced dialysis. Participants must have chronic kidney disease (CKD) and anaemia.

filter_requirements: Database search terms using MEDLINE or EBASE to identify studies on:
- Renal insufficiency/chronic kidney disease
- Anemia (anaemia)
- Recombinant erythropoietin (rHuEPO, EPO, darbepoetin, epoetin, erythropoietin)

other_info: Evaluates the efficacy of rHuEPO in treating anaemia in CKD patients. Includes assessments of:
- Progression to dialysis
- Initiation rates of Renal Replacement Therapy (RRT)
- End-of-study GFR and serum creatinine levels
- Changes in hemoglobin/haematocrit, blood transfusion rates, and quality of life measures like exercise capacity
- Hypertension progression (blood pressure changes) and introduction of antihypertensive treatment
- Adverse events (discharge due to adverse events, access problems, seizures)
- Mortality rates

Additionally, rejection reasons include comparisons between early and late commencement of erythropoietin (EPO).
