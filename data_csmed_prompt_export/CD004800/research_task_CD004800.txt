research_direction: Ankylosing Spondylitis and Spondyloarthropathies  
other_info: This systematic review evaluates the benefits and harms of sulfasalazine for ankylosing spondylitis treatment. It includes 11 studies with 895 participants, comparing sulfasalazine to placebo. The pooled results show a small reduction in pain but significant adverse effects. This systematic review was conducted through Cochrane Central Register of Controlled Trials, MEDLINE, EMBASE, CINAHL, Ovid MEDLINE data, WHO ICTRP. Randomized controlled trials (RCTs) and quasi‐RCTs with sulfasalazine treatment, at least 12 weeks duration, excluding studies not meeting specific disease activity criteria.
