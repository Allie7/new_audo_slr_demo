research_direction: Frailty老年人的护理模型  
Time frame: last two years; Keywords: 整合护理, Frailty老年人, 护理模型, 老年护理实践, 护理效果, 社区; Exclusions: No structured follow-up or designated lead professional  

The review included randomized controlled trials (RCTs) evaluating the care model for integrated care of frailty elderly patients versus usual care. The studies were conducted in community settings across high-, middle-, and low-income countries, utilizing peer-reviewed literature including conference abstracts and unpublished data. Trials were included irrespective of their publication status or language.
