filter_requirements: We included studies if they were randomised controlled trials (RCTs) with double‐blind assessment of participant outcomes following two weeks or more of treatment, although the emphasis of the review was on studies with a duration of eight weeks or longer. We required full journal publication, with the exception of online clinical trial results summaries of otherwise unpublished clinical trials and abstracts with sufficient data for analysis. We excluded short abstracts (usually meeting reports). We did not include non‐randomised studies, studies of experimental pain, case reports, or clinical observations.

Nortriptyline at any dose, by any route, administered for the relief of chronic腰痛, and compared with placebo or any active comparator. The outcomes included patient‐reported pain relief of 30% or greater, 50% or greater, PGIC much or very much improved, and PGIC very much improved. Withdrawals due to lack of efficacy, adverse events, and for any cause were also considered. Participants experiencing any adverse event or serious adverse event were excluded.
