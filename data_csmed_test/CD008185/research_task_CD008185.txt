Intervention: Randomised and quasi‐randomised controlled clinical trials of parallel or cross‐over design comparing enzyme replacement therapy (idursulfase) in any amount given for a period of at least one month to no intervention; placebo; other options (e.g., behavioral strategies, transplantation). Specifically, weekly intravenous administration of idursulfase for more than one year was used as the intervention. Additionally, intradosefulase-IT is being evaluated in children with severe mucopolysaccharidosis as part of a separate Phase I/II clinical trial assessing its safety and dosing requirements.  
Participants: Individuals with Mucopolysaccharidosis II diagnosed by enzyme assay in leukocytes, fibroblasts or plasma using substrates specific for I2S.
